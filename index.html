A Project-Based Learning Framework

DEEDS: A Framework for Learning
That Matters

What if your students spent the school year solving real, critical problems in their community — and mastered core content in the process?

DEEDS is a project-based learning framework that guides students through repeated cycles of investigation, design, and action, each anchored in a genuine community challenge.

Getting Started

Finding Your Community Challenge

A strong community challenge is real, close, and worth solving.

Strategies for Identifying Challenges

👂
Listen to students Students often name the problems closest to them — let their voices lead.
🤝
Partner with community organizations Local nonprofits, city agencies, and advocacy groups carry real, unsolved problems.
📍
Look for the proximal The best challenges are nearby — visible, tangible, and actionable within a school year.

Examples

Improve air quality in our neighborhood
Support foster youth transitions
Redesign schoolyard for safe play
